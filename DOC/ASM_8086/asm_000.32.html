                                                            r r r r r r r r r
IRET
 
          Logic:    POP IP
                    POP CS
                    POPF                          ; Pop flags from stack
 
    IRET returns control from an interrupt routine to the point where the
    interrupt occurred, by popping IP, CS, and the Flag registers.

   no operands                 32         3        1     IRET
